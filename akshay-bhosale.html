Akshay Bhosale

Dancer and Choreographer

Akshay Bhosale is a professional dancer and choreographer for more than a decade. He has trained more than 2000 students worldwide.

With his amazing dancing skills and dedication, Akshay has won over 50 National level prizes for dancing and over 6 International prizes even after the tragic incident after which he had to undergo several surgeries including a total hip replacement surgery.

Akshay has managed to get back physically and mentally to living his life by overcoming the physical restrictions and mental depression with the help of dance! Listen to Akshay on 19th December on TEDxACE Virtual Event as he talks about how dance helped him heal! Also, join Akshay on YouTube and be a part of dance loving family of over 150000 subscribers!
