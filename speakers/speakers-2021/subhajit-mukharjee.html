Subhajit Mukharjee

Founder of Mission Green Mumbai

Subhajit Mukherjee is the founder of Mission Green Mumbai where he inspires citizens to protect, plant and adopt trees with the motto of "Each One, Plant One"​. As an ambassador, he has contributed to the Majhi Vasundhara Abhiyan campaign, an initiative by the Government of Maharashtra, to create a citizen's movement to conserve the environment and biodiversity.

He quit his 12 year old career as a human resource personnel and decided to dedicate his life to making Mumbai greener by urging citizens to adopt at least one native fruit or wood tree and take care of it.

He has been honored as a ‘Jal Rakshak’ and awarded the title of ‘Water Hero’ for his innovations and efforts in the field of water conservation for Jal Shakti Abhiyan under Ministry Of Jal Shakti, New Delhi. The BMC has given him the title of ‘Water Hero of Mumbai’ for educating more than 16,000 students on implementation of Rainwater Harvesting techniques.

His environmental initiatives have been applauded and tweeted by our respected Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
