Haimanti Sen

Founder - Junoon

Haimanti Sen is the Founder of Junoon- an NGO that tutors underprivileged kids to help get their right to education.

She has redefined the phrases ‘age is just a number’ and ‘where there is a will there's a way’ by initiating to teach underprivileged kids at a skywalk in Kandivali at the age of 22.

Her ‘Junoon’ to impart education to the underprivileged is inspirational to many who feel the pangs of compassion but walk on and forget all about trying to help the masses of unfortunate children begging at Mumbai’s signals and stations.
